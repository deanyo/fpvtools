what fpvtools is for

fpvtools is for small Betaflight-heavy tools that do one job properly. The aim is simple: pages you can open fast, use fast, and send to other pilots without having to explain them for five minutes first.

current use case

The first live tool is rates. Compare Actual and Betaflight, check the graph, export CLI, and send someone a real link instead of a cropped configurator screenshot.
